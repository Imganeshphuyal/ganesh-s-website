About Me

Hello! I'm Ganesh Phuyal, a passionate Civil Engineer, Cybersecurity Enthusiast, and Creative Writer from Nepal. My journey began with an early fascination for technology and ethical hacking, which led me to explore operating systems like Kali Linux, Arch Linux, and Parrot OS. I enjoy testing networks, learning cybersecurity tools, and staying updated with the latest in the hacking community.

Alongside my tech interests, I pursued a Diploma in Civil Engineering from Khwopa Polytechnic Institute, affiliated with CTEVT. My field experience includes working as a Quantity Analysis Engineer at the 400-Bed Hospital Project in BPKIHS Dharan. I have hands-on experience in site supervision, quantity estimation, surveying, and project coordination.

Beyond engineering and hacking, I am also deeply passionate about writing stories and poems. Writing allows me to express my thoughts, life experiences, and creative ideas in unique ways. Whether it's a technical project or a piece of literature, I always aim to blend creativity with practical skills.
